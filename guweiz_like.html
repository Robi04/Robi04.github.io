GUWEIZ PIECES

First digital painting I've done :

Since I bought my differents tablets I had only been drawing manga/anime charactere. But with time I disovered many artists with their own style. Guweiz is the first one who truly amazed me with the mood he creates :
  • Dark color
  • Chill mood
  • Asian environnement
So I decided to try painting and not only sketching 2 of his pieces which fascinated me. It took me 5 hours for one and 10 hours for the second one. This is the first time I really enjoyed playing with the colors and discovering how people usually do (their workflow)
When I discovered what you can achieve by painting I even came back to my previous pieces to finish them and give them more of my personnality !
