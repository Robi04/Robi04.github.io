WEBSITE FOR
BOCHU TP
Website to promote the compagny

Context :

During my second year of my first university diploma in computer science, I had to do an internship for 2 months. It was the beginning of the covid, so it was kinda hard to find a compagny to work for. Fortunately my family got me !
Bochu TP is a society specialised in public works (at high altitude). They used to have a website (Wordpress) that was done in 2014. The differents displayed informations where false and the design was not even decent. So I asked them if I could rebuilt it. The goals were :
  • To display the ensentials information to the customers
  • To impress the customers with differents pictures of their works
  • To let the customers the possibility to contact the compagny
  • To create an admin section to change the pictures of each section of the website

Mock-Up :

For the Mock-Up, I had to keep in mind to make something simple. When doing a personnal project you want to show-off your skills and end up doing useless things. The BOCHUTP compagny doesn't have colors that reflect it. The logo is black on white and so I decided to go for this color scheme.
I thought it was a good idea to make one page per activity so the potential customer can see the data he needs easely. That catch the viewer's attention I decided display a huge picture that represents an activity when you click on a page
